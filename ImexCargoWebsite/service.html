Transportation

Logistic services involve the planning, coordination, and execution of transportation activities. This includes selecting the appropriate mode of transportation (e.g., trucks, ships, airplanes, trains) and managing the movement of goods from suppliers to manufacturers and from manufacturers to distributors or retailers.

Warehousing

Warehousing is a vital component of logistics services. It involves the storage of goods in warehouses or distribution centers. These facilities serve as points where inventory can be stored, sorted, and organized before distribution. Efficient warehousing helps in reducing lead times and ensuring product availability.
